Leopard Tech-O

How To Play:

Keep your pet as happy as you can by taking care of it doing the following: Feeding your pet to keep their Hunger scale down, Play with your pet so your pet doesn't get bored to death...literally, and make sure your pet gets Sleep! If any of the attributes reaches 10 your pet dies.
